That's "I _had_ many valuable files ..."

You do have a backup, don't you ?  No ?  Then you're a fool.

mkswap has overwritten the first 15Mb of the partition with zeroes.

It's possible that you can use e2fsck with an alternative superblock
parameter to recover some of the data.  Try -b <n*8192+1>  where n is
a nonnegative integer.  I think you'll have to start with at least
16385, since blocks 1 to 15000 will have been wiped.
