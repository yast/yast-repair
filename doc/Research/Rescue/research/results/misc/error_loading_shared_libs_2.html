I think that's a result of the package being compiled against glibc
2.1 instead of 2.0.  I was seeing that with some packages soon after
glibc 2.1 came out.  One thing to consider is upgrading to 2.1 (which
I've done).  Otherwise, you can probably compile from source or just
go back to an older rpm.
