Hi,
 I've had similar problems behaviour and this is what fixed it for me.
However I saw it on during installs of RHL 6.2 (XFree 3.3.6) & 7.0 (4.0.1?)
on a desktop PC very different from yours.

 My problem was I was trying to enable my scroll button. Problem didn't
exist with my old XF86Config. I followed the advice to change the Protocol
line as appropriate and to add the 'ZAxisMapping 4 5' line. Trouble always
ensued. Never did it mention to change the Buttons line from 3 to 5. Doing
so solved my headaches.

Here's the relevant section of my config file.

Section "InputDevice"
    Identifier  "Mouse1"
    Driver      "mouse"
    Option      "Protocol"      "IMPS/2"
    Option      "Device"        "/dev/mouse"
    Option      "ZAxisMapping"  "4 5"
    Option "Buttons" "5"
EndSection
