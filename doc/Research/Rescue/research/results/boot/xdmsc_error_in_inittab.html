Package xdmsc: Error in /etc/inittab

Deutsch

Support knowledgebase (xdmsc_respawn)
Applies to

SuSE Linux: Version 5.1

Symptom:

After activating the entry for xdmsc in /etc/inittab it is not possible anymore to boot correctly or to change the runlevel.

Cause:

A wrong line crept into /etc/inittab : 

#7:2:+/sbin/init.d/rx tty7
or 
7:2:+/sbin/init.d/rx tty7
The third column with the command, that is responsible for the correct call of the process /sbin/init.d/rx is missing.

Solution:

The correct form of the line is 

#7:2:respawn:+/sbin/init.d/rx tty7
or 
7:2:respawn:+/sbin/init.d/rx tty7
Please change /etc/inittab accordingly
