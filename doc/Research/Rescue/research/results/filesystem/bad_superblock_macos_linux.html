If MacOS 9.1 can see the partition, your best option is the disk repair 
tool "DiskWarrior" from Alsoft.  The Apple supplied utility "Disk First 
Aid" _might_ be able to repair the partition, but usually, failed 
attempts to repair a damaged partion produce additional damage, so I 
would recommend to go with the best first.
