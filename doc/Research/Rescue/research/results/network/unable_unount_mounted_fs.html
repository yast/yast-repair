The only reason for being unable to unmount a mounted filesystem is that
someone is currently using the filesystem.  In your case, it could be
sendmail, someone running mail, someone that has cd'd to the /mailboxes
filesystem, etc.

I mount and unmount nfs filesystems all the time, even using a PPP link
from home, and have no problems.  In fact, I'm currently working on bringing
up automount to make this even simpler as I can avoid any direct mounting
and let amd take care of it...
