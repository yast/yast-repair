Requirements on the Rescue System

  • separate problems and errors must be solved correctly, completely and in an appreciable operating expense,
  • extensibility,
  • modular construction, i.e. separate problems and errors must be solved by self-contained modules,
  • always let the user know what is processed in the background of the Rescue System,
  • always the possibility for the user to intervene into the rescue processes,
  • eventually the possibility to reverse all actions made by the Rescue System,
  • ...
